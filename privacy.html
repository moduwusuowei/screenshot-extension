隐私政策

1. 概述

本隐私政策适用于“一键网页截图助手”浏览器插件（以下简称“插件”），旨在明确插件的隐私保护规则，保障用户个人信息安全，规范数据处理行为。

2. 数据收集说明

本插件仅提供网页截图功能，不主动收集、存储用户任何个人信息，包括但不限于姓名、手机号、地理位置、浏览记录等。

插件运行过程中产生的截图文件，仅存储在用户本地设备，不上传至任何服务器、不与第三方共享，也不进行任何数据统计或分析。

3. 数据存储说明

截图文件仅保存在用户本地设备中，由用户自行管理，插件不进行云端备份、不获取截图内容，也不保留任何截图记录。

4. 隐私保护承诺

1. 不收集用户任何个人信息、浏览记录、设备信息；

2. 不向任何第三方共享、出售、泄露用户相关数据；

3. 不读取用户浏览器缓存、Cookie、表单数据等隐私内容；

4. 插件运行全程无后台数据上传、无日志记录。

5. 政策更新

本隐私政策可能根据相关法律法规及插件功能更新进行调整，更新后将在插件内公示，用户继续使用插件即视为接受更新后的政策。

6. 联系我们

如对隐私保护有疑问，可通过开发者邮箱咨询。

Privacy Policy

1. Overview

This privacy policy applies to the "Web Screenshot Tools" browser extension (hereinafter referred to as the "Extension"). It aims to clarify the privacy protection rules, ensure the security of users' personal information, and regulate data processing behaviors.

2. Data Collection Instructions

The Extension only provides webpage screenshot functionality and does not actively collect or store any user personal information, including but not limited to name, phone number, geographic location, and browsing history.

All screenshot files generated during use are stored only on the user's local device, not uploaded to any server, and not shared with any third parties.

3. Data Storage Instructions

Screenshot files are only stored on the user's local device, managed by the user themselves. The Extension does not perform cloud backup, nor does it access or retain any screenshot records.

4. Privacy Protection Commitment

1. Do not collect any user personal information, browsing history, or device information;

2. Do not share, sell, or disclose any user-related data to any third party;

3. Do not read the user's browser cache, cookies, or form data;

4. No background data upload or log recording during the operation of the Extension.

5. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be adjusted according to relevant laws and regulations and the update of the Extension's functions. Any updates will be announced within the Extension, and continued use of the Extension will be deemed acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about privacy protection, please contact us via the developer's email.
